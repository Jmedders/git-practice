Galvanize & Future Endeavors

Unofficial site for Jeff Medders

Jeffrey grew up in Oklahoma, and graduated from the University of Oklahoma where he studied Ecology and graduated with a B.S. in 2013. He also obtained a minor in Russian Language and plans to backpack across Russia someday.

Having grown tired of the flatlands he decided to move west. Jeff moved to Boulder, CO in early 2014 after living in California for a short time.

In his free time he likes to hike, camp, go to concerts, and play music. He loves plants, bugs, and animals of all kind and is pictured to the right with a ladybug friend.

In the fall of 2015 Jeff and friend, Tim Novak, formed a shoegaze/noise-pop/ambient band called Adult Colour. The duo plans to get in the recording studio in 2016 and play a small number of shows at houses, bars, or anywhere else that will have them.
